FAQ and Troubleshooting

Q. How can I book ?
A. Hotel offer various booking options. Besides our service center, which is available from Monday to Friday, you can contact anytime our hotel directly via email.
Q. How can I subscribe/unsubscribe for the newsletter?
A. You will find a link on our homepage, for example in the footer, where you can subscribe for our newsletter or you can directly follow this link: Newsletter subscription If you wish to unsubscribe in the future, you will find a link at the bottom of each newsletter.
Q. How can I cancel my bookings?
A. Simply drop a mail at our office (mail-id is provided) to cancel our booking.
Q. Can I bring my pet?
A. In our Hotel pets are welcome. Depending on the hotel, there is a fee of 1000-3000INR. Please note that for hygienic reasons pets are usually not allowed in the gastronomic areas.
